## Suffer

As the old song goes: "Do what you want / But don't do or around me". Or the reverse of that, really, if only for the safety and wellbeing of others. 

35 degrees C outside today. Plus some humidity for a heat index of pretty bad. It's a good time to sneak away during lunch for a run. Five kilometers only. Ease into the hotter weather. Feel out what can be handled. Five kilometers isn't too bad for a check run. Suffer, survive, advance. 

Why?

At this stage of the game, why? There's no race to prepare for. No obvious upside to heat training. Plenty of obvious downside though.  Nothing more than an abstract belief that It's Good For You. Build a reserve of toughness. You might need it later. 

I don't know about the utility of that now. But the habit doesn't seem worth breaking. Build a reserve of toughness. I might need it later.
